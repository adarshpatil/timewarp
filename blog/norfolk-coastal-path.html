Travelogue - Norfolk Coastal Path

adarshpatil
2024
Travelogue; England; Norfolk

Last updated: 30th March 2025


After relocating from Scotland to Cambridge, I found myself missing the rhythm of coastal walks and was craving by the lure of wide skies, shifting tides, and the peaceful cadence of the sea. Having previously completed the Fife Coastal Path, I was eager to find a new trail to explore—and the Norfolk Coast Path quickly became my next adventure.
I began walking the Norfolk Coast Path in stages, using weekends as a chance to escape the city. Each segment covered around 15–20 kilometers, with careful planning to drive from Cambridge to the start point of each walk and catch a bus back at the end. The trail spans roughly 140 kilometers, stretching between Hunstanton and Hopton-on-Sea. What follows is an epilogue of that journey—walked piece by piece, in slow silence, and the joy of moving forward.


Stages
1. Hunstanton to Deepdale
2. Deepdale to Wells-next-the-Sea
3. Wells-next-the-Sea to Salthouse

Stage 2: Deepdale to Wells-next-the-Sea: 18km
